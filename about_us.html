Since 2002 Demented Games has reached out into the community in the hopes of activities for all ages. Imagination is all one we have in experiencing a world beyond our own. From traveling the stars to racing in the Indy 500, we want all dreamers.

If all you need is to learn how to play Poker, Texas Hold’em, to Backgammon, or all you want is to understand the games and the terminology that go with them we can help that next trip to the Reno or Vegas. Just call and we can have you come by during the next game or setup some time that fits you.

No matter what, there will always be something to see and do for the courageous adventure. But remember this fellow adventurer, will the path you take be the path of light . . . or the path of shadows. Come on by, give a call, or send an e-mail let's find out together.
